## How to Use

1. **Open the app**: Simply open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Vocabulary Tab**: 
   - Click on flashcards to flip and see translations
   - Use "I Know This" or "Need Practice" buttons to track your progress
   - Navigate with Previous/Next buttons
3. **Practice Tab**: 
   - Click "Start Practice" to begin exercises
   - Answer questions and get immediate feedback
   - Continue with "Next Question" to keep practicing
4. **Common Phrases Tab**: 
   - Browse through essential Spanish phrases
   - Click on cards to interact with them
5. **Statistics Tab**: 
   - View your learning progress
   - See your practice scores
   - Reset progress if needed
